Our Mission: Be the Companion Every Govt Employee Needs

The word "Bandhhu" (બંધુ) means companion or friend in Gujarati. That is exactly what this magazine aims to be — a reliable, knowledgeable friend who keeps you informed about matters that directly affect your service life every month.

Gujarat has over 5 lakh government employees across departments, districts, and talukas. Yet there was no single publication dedicated to their interests — covering transfers, promotions, service rules, RTI rights, pension updates, court judgments, and vigilance matters in one place.

OFFICE BANDHHU was launched in June 2026 to fill that gap. We are registered with the Press Registrar of India (PRGI No. GJGUJ/26/A3280) and are committed to independent, accurate, and useful journalism for Gujarat's government workforce.

Our Editorial Principles

What guides every article we publish

🎯

Accurate & Verified

Every service rule, GR, transfer order, and court judgment we publish is verified from official sources before printing.

🇮🇳

Gujarat-First

We focus exclusively on Gujarat government employees. No national fluff — only what matters to you in your daily service life.

💡

Practical & Useful

Every article answers a real question: "What does this mean for me?" Service rules, rulings, and orders explained plainly.

⚖️

Employee Rights First

We believe every government employee should know their rights — regarding leave, transfer, pension, RTI, and disciplinary matters.

🔍

Vigilance Coverage

ACB cases and corruption matters are covered fairly, factually, and without sensationalism — because accountability matters.

📖

Accessible to All

₹109 per issue ensures that every government employee — from peon to principal secretary — can afford to stay informed.
